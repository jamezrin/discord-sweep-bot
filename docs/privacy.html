Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026  ·  Discord Sweep Bot

Overview

Discord Sweep Bot is designed with privacy in mind. The Bot is completely stateless — it has no database and stores no user data persistently. This page explains what transient information passes through the Bot during normal operation.

1. What Data We Process

When you invoke a command, the following identifiers are transiently processed to fulfil your request and written to operational logs:

  • User IDThe Discord numeric ID of the user who invoked the command.
  • Guild IDThe numeric ID of the Discord server where the command was used.
  • Channel IDsThe numeric IDs of the channels targeted for deletion.
  • TimestampThe date and time the event occurred.
  • Event typeWhether a deletion succeeded or failed.

No message content, usernames, email addresses, IP addresses, or any other personal information is collected or logged.

2. How We Use This Data

  • To fulfill the requested channel deletion operations.
  • To produce operational logs for debugging, error investigation, and abuse monitoring.

We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the Bot.

3. Data Retention

Operational logs are retained only as long as needed for debugging and monitoring purposes, after which they are discarded. No data is stored in a database or persistent volume — the Bot container holds no state between restarts.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. Log data is not disclosed to outside parties except where required by applicable law.

5. Discord's Role

The Bot operates via the Discord API. Interactions with the Bot are also subject to Discord's Privacy Policy. We have no control over the data Discord itself collects as part of its platform.

6. Open Source

The Bot's full source code is available at github.com/jamezrin/discord-sweep-bot under the GPL-3.0 license. You can verify exactly what data is processed by reading the code, and you may self-host the Bot if you prefer to operate it yourself.

7. Your Rights

Because the Bot stores no persistent personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have concerns about data processed in operational logs, please contact us via GitHub and we will address your request.

8. Children's Privacy

The Bot does not knowingly collect data from users under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). Use of the Bot requires a Discord account, which is governed by Discord's own age restrictions.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the Bot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please open an issue at github.com/jamezrin/discord-sweep-bot/issues.
